About Us

Scrap Metal Kenya LLC is Kenya's and East Africaâ€™s oldest and largest metal recycling company. For the last century, the company has purchased, collected, processed and recycled all forms of ferrous metals (iron and steel) and non-ferrous metals (aluminium, copper, zinc, stainless steel, lead, nickel, brass, tin, bronze and others).

We are founding member of Kenya Scrap Metal Traders Association.

From our conveniently-located scrap yards in and around Nairobi, Mombasa and Kisumu, we purchase scrap metals from a range of Kenyan sources including industrial enterprises, public entities, scrap metal dealers and private individuals. In addition, our company acquires scrap metal from off-site demolition projects, where it is able to provide expert demolition services to mines and factories. It is fully equipped to deal with all environmental hazards including radioactive material and asbestos. We are fully committed to preserving the fragile environment in which we live and to dealing with all materials in an environmentally responsible manner. We pride ourselves on our impeccable health and safety record throughout the Group.

Scrap Metal Kenya LLC is a proudly Kenyan company and has a long tradition of trading in most Eastern African countries including Uganda, Tanzania, Rwanda, Sudan, Ethiopia, Mozambique, and DRC - The Democratic Republic of the Congo.
